# Regional Sales & Profit Analysis (Superstore Dataset)

---

## Short Project Overview

This project analyzes sales and profitability across U.S. regions using the Superstore dataset.  
The analysis focuses on identifying top-performing states, key profit drivers, and underperforming segments across regions.

## Key Business Questions

- **Which regions and states generate the highest sales and profit?**  
  The West region leads in total sales and profit, driven primarily by California.

- **Do high sales always translate to high profitability?**  
  Not always â€” the Central region has strong sales but low profit efficiency, especially in the Consumer segment.

- **Which segments and product categories drive or reduce profit?**  
  Technology consistently drives profit across regions, while Furniture shows strong sales but recurring losses.

---

## Key Insights

- The West region leads in both total sales and profitability, driven primarily by California.  
- The Central region shows high sales volume but weak profit efficiency, particularly in the Consumer segment.  
- Technology is the most consistent profit-driving category across regions.  
- Furniture records strong sales but recurring profit losses, especially in the Central region.  
- The South region, while currently the smallest, shows the strongest projected growth rate.
